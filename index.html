Welcome to Royal Caribbean® Help Desk

At Royal Caribbean International™, we understand that planning a cruise vacation should be as relaxing as the vacation itself. Our Help Desk is staffed with knowledgeable professionals ready to assist you with every aspect of your cruise experience.

Whether you're booking your first cruise or you're a seasoned Crown & Anchor® Society member, our team is here to ensure your journey is seamless from start to finish. We offer support for reservations, itinerary changes, special accommodations, onboard activities, and any other questions you may have about your Royal Caribbean® adventure.

Our Help Desk operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, because we know travel questions don't always happen during business hours. With just one call to 1-888-230-8925, you'll be connected with a cruise specialist who can provide personalized assistance tailored to your specific needs.

Royal Caribbean International™ takes pride in offering world-class service both on our ships and through our support channels. When you call our Help Desk, you're not just getting answers - you're getting peace of mind knowing that your cruise vacation is in the hands of experts who care about making your experience extraordinary.

From technical support with our Royal Caribbean app to last-minute booking changes, dietary restrictions, or accessibility needs, no request is too big or small for our dedicated team. We're here to turn your cruise dreams into reality, one call at a time.
